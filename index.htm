Hello there freinds of Michael Knight,
I am here to help you explore your race legs, exchange locations, and view vital inforamtion needed for your mission.

Below are a few tips to help you navigate the map:

- click a name in the sidebar (or an exchange number on the map) to zoom to that location and view info

- click on the map to hide the info window

- click K.I.T.T to zoom out to view the whole course

- toggle the basemap by using the top-right buttons

- use the fancy compass to rotate and pivet the map view (it's like you're driving a drone)

- use scroll wheel on mouse to zoom in/out of map

- click on Race Legs title to open/close runner lists
